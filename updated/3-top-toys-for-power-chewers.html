Dr. Teagan's Top 3 Toys for Power Chewers

LAST UPDATED 15 MAY 2020

Written by Pet Circle veterinarian, Dr Teagan Lever BVSc (Hons)

As the owner of two staffies, I can attest that there is nothing more frustrating than spending your hard earned pay on some new toys, only to find them chewed to bits after 15 minutes. Some dogs just chew more than others, so how can you keep them happy without breaking the bank? It may be tempting to buy cheaply made toys, however these are unlikely to go the distance. While no toy is indestructible, I can highly recommend these 3 toys as being tough, durable, and built to go the distance for power chewers.

Top 3 Toys for Power Chewers

1. Kong Extreme - Made from an ultra-durable, all-natural rubber, the KONG Extreme also has an erratic bounce, making it perfect for fetch and playtime as well. Stuff with peanut butter or treats to keep your dog occupied and help beat boredom and separation anxiety. For extra destructive power chewers, I recommend purchasing a size or two larger than recommended by the manufacturer.

KONG Extreme

Tasty Bone

2. Tasty Bone - This durable, flavoured bone is made of nylon and designed to keep even the most persistent and powerful of chewers satisfied. It can be washed in soap and water if needed and the flavour is designed to last for the lifetime of the bone. For my staffies, each one lasts a couple of months.

3. KONG Wobbler - This durable toy can be filled with kibble or treats and left to entertain your dog all morning! It sits upright until nudged, dispensing tasty rewards as it wobbles, spins and rolls. The unpredictable movement stimulates natural instincts. And, if you have a dog that tends to eat too fast, you can use the KONG Wobbler as an alternative to a dog bowl to help extend mealtime.
