Black Hawk Updates

LAST UPDATED 27th JANUARY 2024

Over the next few months you'll notice some changes to the Black Hawk cat food range.

After extensive research and development Black Hawk have improved the formulation and benefits of their dry and wet cat food diets.

Changes to the Black Hawk range include:

  • - the addition of beneficial ingredients such as increased protein, Yucca, chicken liver, fish oil and glucosamine
  • - increased selection of dry and wet food options
  • - development of the new Healthy Benefits range, catering to cats' specific needs
  • - updated packaging and changes to pack sizes
  • - change in cost
  • - discontinuation of some dry food formulations

    What do I need to do?

    Ensure a gradual transition

    Because the ingredients in some of these products have changed, to avoid any dietary upsets, we recommend that you gradually transition your cat from the old to new formula over a period of 7 to 10 days.

    For detailed instructions on how to transition your pet's food, please see How to Introduce a New Food to Your Pet.

    If you have any questions or concerns, you can contact our Pet Circle Vet Squad via email or via live chat on the Pet Circle website.
