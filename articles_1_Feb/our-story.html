Our Story

Like many good stories, Pet Circle's began with a man and his dog. One day Mike noticed that his dad had stopped buying their family's very handsome golden retriever - George - his favourite thing: big, 15 kilogram bags of delicious food, after injuring his back!

After a bit of thought, Mike realised that his dad wasn't the only person out there who found the process of driving to the pet store, picking up a huge bag of dog food, and hauling it home to be difficult and time-consuming. So, he thought of a way to tackle the problem. And so, Pet Circle was born - with the aim of helping people care for their pets by making purchasing pet products as simple as possible.

Meet Our Leadership Team

Michael Frizell

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

From an engineering and finance background, Mike spent his early years working in the Management Consulting and Investment Banking industries for companies like Macquarie Group, Greenhill & Co and GEM Consulting.

Mike co-founded Pet Circle in 2011, inspired after seeing his father struggle to carry large bags of dog food home from the store. As CEO, he is responsible for setting the strategy and direction for Pet Circle overall, and for ensuring we have the resources to deliver on our vision.

"I am passionate about changing how pet parents globally care for their pets. We are building a world-class data and technology-enabled relationship commerce company at Pet Circle."
